Features

  • Boards

    Projects are represented as boards in the system. Along with displaying essential information about the project, they can be set to public or private, depending on the user's perferences.

  • Tasks

    Tasks and subtasks are the smallest chunks of executable assignments. Tasks can be assigned to multple users, given a priority and easily moved across different states in the devolopment pipline.

  • Members

    Users invited to work together with the common goal of delivering a successfull project. They can have several different roles as active members of the devolopment, or simply followers of the board.

  • Timer

    The timer tracking feature enables the users to easily measure the time spent on a specific task. Once tracked and logged, the time can be seen and edited in a dedicated Time Records view for additional control.

  • Follow

    Ahead offers the possibility to follow the devolopment of a project and be notified of its activities. Users can follow entire boards,or they can choose to follow just specific tasks within a board.

  • Recently Accessed

    The boards that you are actively working on can be easily accessed with the Recently Accessed feature.
